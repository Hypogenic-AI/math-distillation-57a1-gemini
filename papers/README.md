1. [The Equational Theories Project: Advancing Collaborative Mathematical Research at Scale](2512.07087_Equational_Theories_Project.pdf)
   - Authors: Bolan et al.
   - Year: 2025
   - arXiv: 2512.07087
   - Why relevant: This is the definitive paper on the project that generated the 22M implications being used in the Mathematics Distillation Challenge. It provides the numbering system, key laws, and the implication graph structure.

## Summary of Key Papers
The project successfully determined all implications between 4694 simplest equational laws on magmas. The laws are numbered E1 to E4694. E1 is the trivial law ($x = x$), and E2 is the singleton law ($x = y$). Most other laws are combinations of the binary operation $\diamond$ and variables $x, y, z, w$.
